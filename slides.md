# Why Robotics Education?

- Teaches problem decomposition

- Integrates mechanics, electronics, and software

- Students learn best by doing

- Provide challenging, meaningful problems

- Hands‑on learning is essential

# GVC Concept

- **Not** a shared lab (focus on shared hardware access)

- Joint courses between NTNU (Taiwan) and Ostfalia (Germany)

- Students collaborate across cultures

- Focus on teamwork + robotics challenges

- 2024 - 2026

# Bamboleo Game (2024)

- 30 NTNU + 25 Ostfalia students

- Joint lectures + local lectures

- Group project: robot that plays Bamboleo

# Tasks in Bamboleo

- Camera calibration, Board pose estimation, Piece detection & localization, Strategy selection, Motion planning & manipulation

- Most groups detected and picked pieces 
    + 2/3 tilted boards
    + 1/3 play game

- Students enjoyed collaboration
